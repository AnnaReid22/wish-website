WELCOME

WISH (Women Involved in Software & Hardware) is a support group for female computing majors at the California Polytechnic State University in San Luis Obispo. Founded in 2007, WISH is still a young community that continues to grow each year. Our goal is to empower our technical women and rectify the gender gap in the computing field.

2022 Summer Spotlights

Mansi Achuthan

4TH YEAR | COMPUTER SCIENCE MAJOR

I am a 4th Computer Science major pursuing minors in Data Science and Statistics. I have been a member of WISH all four years here at Cal Poly, and I am currently an officer on the WISH mentorship team. I love of the arts, so some of my hobbies include dancing, drawing, and making mashups of my favorite songs. I was a software engineer at Google for 3 months this summer in San Francisco. I worked under Google Commerce and spent my summer contributing to the backend migration of a production database. It was great to finally have an internship in person and live on my own in a big city. Making friends, eating good food, and exploring different parts of SF on the weekend made my experience really fun.
