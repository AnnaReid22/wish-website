WELCOME

WISH (Women Involved in Software & Hardware) is a support group for female computing majors at the California Polytechnic State University in San Luis Obispo. Founded in 2007, WISH is still a young community that continues to grow each year. Our goal is to empower our technical women and rectify the gender gap in the computing field.

Summer Spotlights


Kylene Landenberger

4TH YEAR | COMPUTER SCIENCE MAJOR

At Cal Poly I have been a part of CPSEC and Cal Poly Racing, currently in WISH, and starting to be involved with CPUX. Outside of academics and clubs I am always keeping myself busy with entrepreneurial projects, training my new puppy Waylon, or hiking. I am excited to be on campus and involved more than I was able to online, for my last year at Cal Poly! I had a great experience interning at VMware remotely for 12 weeks this summer! I was hired for the role of Software Engineering Intern on the Remote Experience team. My team's focus was to enhance the Microsoft Teams software built for our Horizon Client VDI. My role as intern was to create a solution for understanding call failures since it was information Microsoft did not actively release to my team. To do this I intercepted voice and audio call data to then develop a cross-platform native application using webRTC to display understandable diagnostics that helped both the developers on my team and customer service aid in call failures. I got a puppy!!!!!! He has grown up so much already. Also - finally met all my friends that I've been talking to online for over a year now. That was amazing. We went on to the bluff trail at MDO.
