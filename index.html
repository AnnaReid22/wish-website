WELCOME

WISH (Women Involved in Software & Hardware) is a support group for female computing majors at the California Polytechnic State University in San Luis Obispo. Founded in 2007, WISH is still a young community that continues to grow each year. Our goal is to empower our technical women and rectify the gender gap in the computing field.

Summer Spotlights

Tessa Tapmongkol

3RD YEAR | COMPUTER SCIENCE MAJOR

I like to play tennis, hike, lay in my hammock, and I've been learning how to climb! I'm in WISH, Hack4Impact, and Club Tennis. This summer, I interned at Microsoft as an Explore Intern for 12 weeks. I interned for Clarity (a web analytics tool) on the frontend team. I spent 4 weeks doing program management where I learned about the PM mindset and how to write specs/documentation. And for the 8 weeks, I was able to do dev work and push some of my code into production! I camped at Mount Rainier for a few days where I did the Skyline and Burroughs Trail. I also took a day trip to Oregon where I explored Portland and visited Cannon Beach. At the end of the summer, I road tripped from Seattle to SLO where I stopped again in Portland, explored San Francisco, and went down the PCH from the Bay to SLO (so many cute seals!)

Anna Makarewicz
