About WISH

We are women. Technical women. WISH, Women Involved in Software and Hardware is a club that was founded in 2007 by women of the Computer Science Department at California Polytechnic State University, San Luis Obispo.
Our mission is to provide a community of support for females in computing majors and minors, and continuously put effort towards rectifying the gender gap in computing majors and minors and evaluating the systemic problem.
