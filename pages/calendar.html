Events
General Meetings occur every other Thursday at 11am. Join us in room 14-249 (Pilling) to keep up with WISH happenings, socialize with fellow member, play games for prizes, and eat free pizza!
Study Sessions & Pass Parties are a designated space for WISH members to prepare for the most stressful parts of each quarter: midterms, finals, and registration! Join us for great food, good company, and helpful resources.
Lean In Circles with WISH are meetings dedicated to discussing important aspects of being women in tech. Previous topics include allyship, imposter syndrome, and more.
Networking & Talks with Industry provide members with the opportunity to learn about industry and make connections with potential employers. Food is often provided!
Workshops on resume building, LinkedIn, and tech help provide members with the resources to get the internships and jobs they want!
Other events such as movie nights, game nights, and holiday parties are musts as well!
